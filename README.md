# Easeit

Easit is small Blender addon for quickly applying easing presets to animation keyframes.

### Installation
Go to the [Releases page](https://github.com/andycuccaro/easeit-addon/releases) and download according to your Blender version. 
Open Blender, go to _Edit > Preferences > Add-ons_, press the _Install_ button, browse your downloaded addon zip file, and click _Install Add-on_.

### Location
Select an object that has at least two keyframes and Easeit is accessible on:
- **Blender 2.80+**: Graph Editor or Dope Sheet > Sidebar > Easeit
